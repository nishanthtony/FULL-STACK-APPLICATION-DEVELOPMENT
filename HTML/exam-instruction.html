Exam Instructions

Please read the following rules and guidelines carefully before starting the exam. Failure to comply may result in disqualification.

Exam Duration

The exam will last for 60 minutes. You must complete all questions within this time frame. The timer will be displayed during the exam.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Ensure you are in a quiet, well-lit environment.
  • Do not use any unauthorized materials or devices.
  • Proctoring will monitor your session visually.
  • Any suspicious activity may lead to exam termination.

Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s

  • Keep your camera and microphone enabled.
  • Stay within the camera's view at all times.
  • Report any technical issues immediately.

Don’ts

  • Do not leave the room during the exam.
  • Do not communicate with others.
  • Do not attempt to cheat or use external help.

By proceeding, you agree to these terms.
